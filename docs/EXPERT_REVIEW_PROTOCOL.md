# Expert Jyotish Review Protocol

## Purpose

Use this review to approve formulas and rule interpretations, not to claim
scientific prediction accuracy.

- Reviewer name:

## Calculation Review

For each module, record `approved`, `change required`, or `not reviewed`:

- Lahiri sidereal positions and mean-node convention.
- Ascendant and whole-sign house mapping.
- Nakshatra and pada boundaries.
- Vimshottari Mahadasha, Antardasha, and Pratyantar boundaries.
- D2, D3, D4, D7, D9, D10, D12, and D30 mappings.
- Dignity, retrograde, combustion, and vargottama rules.
- Parashari graha drishti.
- Jaimini-style rashi drishti as a separate convention.
- House lordships, functional roles, and dispositors.
- Ashtakavarga totals and house mapping.
- Yoga conditions, cancellation evidence, and caveats.
- Transit ingress, egress, and station windows.

## Interpretation Review

- No rule is presented more strongly than its evidence supports.
- Disputed conventions are named.
- Disabled vargas are never inferred.
- Scores are not described as probabilities.
- Medical, legal, financial, and fear-based claims are excluded.
- Birth-time sensitivity is visible before house or varga conclusions.

## Discrepancy Log

For every disagreement, retain:

1. Module and rule identifier.
2. Serenova output.
3. Reviewer or comparison output.
4. Exact settings and source edition.
5. Root cause.
6. Resolution and regression test.

Approval must not be recorded while unexplained core-position or dasha-boundary
discrepancies remain.
